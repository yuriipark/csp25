Build an Ice Cream

In our game, we gave the user different options to create their own ice cream. You choose between a cup or a cone, how many scoops you want, chocolate or vanilla, and if you want a cherry.


Leprechaun Maze

Try to get the leprechaun to the pot of gold using arrow keys. You will go back to the start if you hit a trap. The leaderboard will appear at the end.


Atari Game

Enter your name and use W, A, S, and D keys to play atari and get a message at the end for a children's hospital card.


Ice Drop

We used scratch to make our game Ice Drop. You try to move the block and drop smaller blocks onto it without missing. If you miss you lose and can restart the game.


Number Trivia

Choose an animal and answer math questions correctly to get the animal to the apple on the other side of the screen. If you get the answer wrong, the animal will tell you the correct answer and go to a different question. This is my AP test project in scratch.
